The Help Squard -
The Help Squad is a unique consultancy service company that was founded by professionals from the interior design industry. Their main goal is to provide customers with better knowledge and support throughout their journey of creating their dream home. The company has a team of highly trained technical experts who interact with clients on a personal level and offer unbiased assistance in understanding technical terminology used by contractors, vendors, and interior design companies. The Help Squad aims to provide customers with the extra subject knowledge expertise they need when dealing with any service provider to complete their dream home.
Our Founder
Meet Mohammed Akram, an engineer turned interior designer with eight years of experience and over 600 completed projects. He has worked for well-known brands and owned his own studio. Tired of the interior design industry's constant trust breaches, Mohammed decided to empower end-users by providing them with the right tools, knowledge, and information to make informed decisions. He's passionate about making a difference and helping others, so feel free to reach out and chat about his journey or share yours.
